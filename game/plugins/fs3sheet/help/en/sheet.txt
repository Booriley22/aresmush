The %xhsheet%xn command lets you view your character sheet.  This contains your character profile, abilities and background.

Some games may allow you to view other character's sheets.  This information is provided for OOC reference; do not use it inappropriately if you have no IC reason to know it.

    %xcsheet%xn - Views your own sheet.
    %xcsheet <name>%xn - Views someone else's sheet.
